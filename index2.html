Many museums and art galleries can be found in the city, including the Swiss National Museum and Kunsthaust. Schauspielhaus Zürich is considered to be one of the most important theatres in the German-speaking world.


Review

I was able to spend 3 wonderful days in Zurich. The city is absolutely beautiful and walking-friendly. The views of the mountains are breathtaking, and there are adorable cafes to grab a bite to eat and enjoy a delicious cappuccino. There are also lovely specialty stores to get unique souvenirs. One such shop sold different liquors and wines out of barrels in which you filled your own glass bottle.

I was also lucky enough to enjoy the annual Street Parade in Zurich. If you are a fan of house music, this event is perfect. We purchased tickets to enjoy it on a Love Mobile parading through Zurich. The crowd and atmosphere was amazing; everyone is so festive and enjoying themselves. Also, at such an event with alcohol, nobody was acting out of order or causing a scene.

I would have like to have stayed longer in Zurich to do some more shopping. I was lucky to find nice clothing stores and pick out a few items. The fashion there is good and not too different than what you find in the States.

Visit Details

Date of Visit: May 2022

Rating: 4/5

Safety: Zurich sees very little tourist-directed crime; however, pickpocketing is common on public transportation and at airports, so keep an eye on your belongings at all times.
